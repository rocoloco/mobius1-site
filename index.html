Product

What Yave does

01

Instant WhatsApp response

Leads receive a reply in seconds, not hours. Yave handles the initial conversation naturally in Spanish or English — indistinguishable from a skilled agent.

02

Natural language property search

AI searches your inventory using natural language. "2 bedroom in Salamanca under €2,000" just works — no dropdowns, no filters.

03

Automated viewing booking

Checks agent availability via Google Calendar and books viewings automatically. No back-and-forth email chains required.

04

Smart agent notifications

Agents are notified only when something truly actionable happens — a hot lead, a qualified buyer, a viewing request. Zero noise.

05

Neighbourhood intelligence

Answers questions about local schools, transport, restaurants, and commute times using live data. Buyers get the full picture instantly.

06

Property feed sync

Automatically syncs listings from Kyero, Idealista, and other portals. Your AI always has the most current inventory — no manual updates.

Process

From zero to live in under an hour

01 /
We set up your WhatsApp

We provision a dedicated WhatsApp Business number for your agency with your brand name as the display name. Nothing technical required from you.

02 /
Sync your properties

Connect your property feed (Kyero XML or similar) and we import your listings with photos, descriptions, and location data automatically.

03 /
Add a button to your site

Place a WhatsApp button on your website. When leads click it, they immediately start a conversation with your AI assistant.

04 /
Yave handles the rest

AI qualifies leads, searches properties, answers questions, and books viewings. Your agents focus on closing deals — not chasing enquiries.
