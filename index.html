What Yave Does

AI-powered WhatsApp automation built specifically for real estate agencies in Spain.

💬

Instant WhatsApp Response

Leads get a response in seconds, not hours. Yave handles the initial conversation naturally in Spanish or English.

🏠

Property Search

AI searches your property inventory using natural language. "2 bedroom in Salamanca under 2000" just works.

📅

Viewing Booking

Checks agent availability via Google Calendar and books viewings automatically. No back-and-forth needed.

🔔

Agent Notifications

Agents get notified when something actionable happens — a hot lead, a viewing request, a qualified buyer.

Neighborhood Intelligence

🔄

Feed Sync

How It Works

From Zero to Live in Under an Hour

Getting started is simple. No technical setup required from the agency.

1

We Set Up Your WhatsApp

We provision a dedicated WhatsApp number for your agency with your brand name as the display name.

2

Sync Your Properties

Connect your property feed (Kyero XML or similar) and we import your listings with photos, descriptions, and location data.

3

Add the Link to Your Website

Place a WhatsApp button on your website. When leads click it, they start a conversation with your AI assistant.

4

AI Handles the Rest

Yave qualifies leads, searches properties, answers questions, and books viewings. Your agents focus on closing deals.
